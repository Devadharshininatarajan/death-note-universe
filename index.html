About Death Note

Death Note is a legendary psychological thriller anime series that explores morality, justice, and the consequences of power. The story follows Light Yagami, a high school genius who discovers a mysterious notebook — the Death Note — which allows him to kill anyone simply by writing their name in it.

Guided by the god of death Ryuk, Light sets out to cleanse the world of evil under the alias Kira. But as his actions grow darker, a world-renowned detective known only as L begins a chilling game of cat and mouse.

With twists at every turn, Death Note challenges its viewers to question what true justice really is. Are you ready to enter the mind of a genius and decide where you stand?
